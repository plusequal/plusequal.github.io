Workshop Overview

All of our += workshops follow the same format. An opening presention from a company that has implemented the technology, telling us about the wins and losses they faced transitioning or implementing the new technology. Followed by a talk on the technology itself, curated to inspire you on what you can create and do with the technology, showcasing it's power. Finally an afternoon workshop where the teacher will show you what we will create, walk you through creating it, then leave you to expand on your creation with them available to help. Wrapping everything up with a final talk from them on next step to complete your project.
