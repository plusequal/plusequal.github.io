Workshop 1pm - 4pm

The Workshop portion is designed to give you the foundations of mobile app development using exciting technologies like React Native, Redux, Firebase, Third Party APIs and more. The skills that you will learn in this workshop can help you pursue the app idea you’ve dreamt of building, stay up to date on the newest mobile technologies, or impress your tech friends by releasing IOS and Android app simultaneously with minimal effort.

In this workshop, you will be guided through the process of building a personalized gif based curation app with data from everyone's favorite gif source — Giphy ! You will be implementing React Native to build the Interface (Views), Redux to handle the data flow, GIPHY API as your data source and integrate with Firebase as your persistent store to keep track of your likes and dislikes for the years to come.
